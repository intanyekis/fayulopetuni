Kehlani Responds To Game's Drunk Instagram Rant

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

After The Game posted (then deleted) what can only be explained as an alcohol induced rant, full of free-flowing and not quite understandable thoughts, singer-songwriter Kehlani took to Instagram to respond to the Game’s comments.

In the post, Game asks his one time friend turned foe 50 Cent to spot him some bottles of his Effen Vodka after admittedly running through 40 bottles of P.Diddy’s Apple Ciroc. The ask was a bit eyebrow raising considering Game and Mr. Jackson haven’t been on the best of term in a decade, but what upset Kehlani was Game referencing her privates as a way to articulate his once close friendship with the Power executive producer.

“Me and the n—a used to be tight as Kehlani p—y, (and no I haven’t, I’m just guessing it’s tight.” Game wrote.

The obviously crass comment didn’t sit well the Grammy nominee and she took to Twitter to air her grievance.

Using a classic photo of Rihanna’s wicked side eye to drive home her disgust, Kehlani made her disdain for Game’s inappropriate comments known.

“Your lack of respect for women is showing,” the 20-year-old Bay Area native said. “Keep my youthful ass name outcha mouth.” she said.

Game, who may very well be hung over hasn’t seen Kehlani’s response, but when he does stay tuned.
