The Meaning Behind The Song: Its Still Rock &amp; Roll to Me (Live at Madison Square Garden, New York

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 19, 2024
TitleArtistWriter/ComposerAlbumRelease DateGenreProducer
It’s Still Rock & Roll to MeBilly JoelBilly Joel12 Gardens Live (2006)2006Pop/RockSteve Lillywhite & Billy Joel

The song “It’s Still Rock & Roll to Me” by Billy Joel holds a special place in the hearts of many music enthusiasts. Released in 1980 and performed live at Madison Square Garden in 2006, this track showcases Joel’s distinctive musical style and lyrical prowess.

As someone who grew up listening to Billy Joel’s music, “It’s Still Rock & Roll to Me” has always resonated with me. The song carries a profound message about staying true to oneself and not succumbing to the constantly changing trends and fads of the music industry.

In the lyrics, Joel addresses the criticism and pressure artists face to conform to the latest fashion or style. He questions why someone’s choice of clothes or car should determine their worth as a musician. Joel argues that no matter the external judgments, it’s the music that truly matters. He states, “Funny, but it’s still rock and roll to me.”

The song tackles the notion of being “out of touch” with the current music scene and emphasizes that rock and roll has never lost its essence, despite the emergence of new genres and trends. Joel aptly sings, “Hot funk, cool punk, even if it’s old junk, it’s still rock and roll to me.” This line celebrates the timelessness of rock and roll, highlighting its persistent relevance and impact.

Personally, this song takes me back to my own teenage years when I was heavily influenced by the music of my parents’ generation. I vividly remember blasting “It’s Still Rock & Roll to Me” in my room, feeling an immense sense of connection to the lyrics. At a time when I often felt misunderstood by my peers for my musical preferences, this song reassured me that my love for rock music was not misplaced or outdated.

Billy Joel’s energetic performance of “It’s Still Rock & Roll to Me” at Madison Square Garden in 2006 adds a new layer of excitement and nostalgia to the track. The live version captures the electric atmosphere of the concert, allowing listeners to experience the power of Joel’s music in a live setting.

The song serves as a reminder to never allow societal pressure or changing trends to dictate one’s authenticity. It encourages listeners to embrace their unique tastes and passion for rock and roll, regardless of the opinions of others.

In conclusion, “It’s Still Rock & Roll to Me” by Billy Joel is a timeless anthem that celebrates the unwavering spirit of rock music. Through its engaging lyrics and memorable melody, the song reminds us that the true essence of music lies in its ability to connect, inspire, and endure. So, whether you’re a fan of old classics or the latest hits, take a moment to appreciate the significance of rock and roll in shaping the musical landscape of our lives.
