Iggy Azalea Responds to Butt Implant Rumors: Watch

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 23, 2024

A man dared to ask Iggy Azalea if her butt is real during the upcoming Thursday, August 11, episode of MTV’s Wild ‘N Out — and he lived to hear her response.

“I just want to say I think you’re real beautiful,” a member of Wild ’N Out’s Platinum Team tells the “Black Widow” rapper in a preview of next week’s episode. “Myself, my castmates and every dude in here would really like to know how much of that booty is really real.”

Azalea took a moment to compose her response before sassily schooling the man.

“Y’all wanna know? These guys over here?” she asked him. “I’m gonna put you up on game: It’s real in your hands, but y’all don’t need to be worrying about booty that’s above your weight class.”

Her response was met with a mic-drop reaction from the crowd.

The brave Platinum Team member isn’t the only one to speculate about Azalea’s famous derriere.

“Iggy got her bum done🍑,” an Instagram follower commented on the singer’s latest booty-baring Instagram photo. In the snap posted on Monday, August 8, the Australian star sits on the edge of a pool in an itty-bitty bikini. “There is no way that is real,” another Instagram user commented, while a third called Azalea out for looking “out of proportion.”

The MC isn’t shy to admit that she’s gone under the knife to tweak other parts of her bod, however. During an April 2016 interview with Ellen DeGeneres, the “Pretty Girls” songstress revealed that she’s had both her nose and breasts done.
