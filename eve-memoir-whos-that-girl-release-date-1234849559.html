Eve's New Memoir, 'Who's That Girl?', Coming This September

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024

Eve is finally telling her story in the form of a new memoir.

Aptly named after one of her hit singles, Who’s That Girl? is slated for release on Sept. 17 and is co-written by journalist and author Kathy Iandoli.

“I’m excited for people to get to know me on a deeper level,” said the Ruff Ryders’ First Lady in a statement. “This story is decades in the making.”

According to PEOPLE, the memoir is described as “fearless, empowering, and inspirational.” It will explore her rise to prominence as a pioneering female emcee, her impact on pop culture and fashion, and the struggle to balance her personal life and professional hustle.

The book won’t gloss over the West Philly native’s journey to Hollywood and will reveal stories on how she became one of the top female rappers in a male-dominated industry, including previously untold recollections behind her discography, “the internal battle leading up to Lip Lock,” how she entered the scene as “Eve of Destruction,” how she made history as the first artist to win a Grammy for Best Rap-Sung Collaboration with Gwen Stefani for “Let Me Blow Ya Mind,” and becoming the third female rapper to have her solo debut on top of the Billboard 200.

Eve will also detail her romance with husband Maximillion Cooper and fertility obstacles. She welcomed her son, Wilde Wolf, in February 2022.

Throughout her career, the 45-year-old has released four albums, costarred in 15 films, starred in her own sitcom as well as Queens—alongside Brandy, Naturi Naughton, and Nadine Velazquez—and co-hosted the Emmy-nominated talk show, The Talk, for three years.
