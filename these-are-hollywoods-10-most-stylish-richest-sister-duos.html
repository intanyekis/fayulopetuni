These Are Hollywood's 10 Most Stylish &amp; Richest Sister Duos

While the pressure of being in the limelight and receiving constant media attention can be a daunting side of Hollywood, some celebrities find comfort when their siblings are with them to shoulder the pressure and help them. Some of the most potent sister duos in Hollywood have a plethora of talent showcased through fashion, acting careers, business ventures, and music. They have gained success in their respective fields by supporting each other through thick and thin. These tenacious women prove that the right encouragement and a strong bond with the closest people in their family can lead to success and stardom.

From the Hadid sisters dominating the world of fashion modeling to the Olsen twins and also Fanning sisters bagged significant roles in movies and television shows.

Tia And Tamera Mowry

Since birth, the twin sisters who have been best friends, Tia and Tamera Mowry, gained an early start in Hollywood as the siblings starred in sitcoms during the mid-1990s. According to Insider, they were the lead actors on the show Sister, Sister and played twins who were separated at birth and later reunited. The siblings have been a part of the separate and combined projects over the years and have an overall net worth of $4 million.

Elle And Dakota Fanning

Elle and Dakota Fanning, one of the youngest and most talented sister duos on the list, have been starring in movies. Dakota gained popularity as a seven-year-old who starred alongside Sean Penn in I Am Sam, while Elle earned a spotlight in 2011. The sisters have made a hefty fortune over the years, $6 million and $12 million, respectively.

Kate And Rooney Mara

Kate Mara is known for her stunning work in television shows like 24 and House Of Cards and has amassed a net worth of $14 million, while Rooney Mara is an Academy Award-nominated actress known for her performances in independent movies with a $16 million fortune, as reported by IMDB. The sisters have had different paths in Hollywood, and they constantly support each other and stand up together for animal rights.

Gigi And Bella Hadid

Carrying on the family history of being in the fashion industry, Gigi and Bella Hadid took the path to becoming professional models. The sisters began their careers young as child models for high-end brand campaigns before their debut in the world of fashion in their teens. The sisters are the most stylish pair known for their love and support of each other. Over the last decade, Gigi and Bella Hadid have amassed a combined net worth of $45 million.

Miley Cyrus And Noah Cyrus

The famous musician Billy Ray Cyrus as their father, Miley and Noah Cyrus, have grown up in the public attention. Their careers have gained them $160 million and $3 million fortunes, respectively. Miley Cyrus first gained popularity as a child actress when she starred in the hit musical show Hannah Montana. Noah Cyrus, her younger sibling, has a slow yet steady career in music at just 21 years of age.

Jessica And Ashlee Simpson

Jessica and Ashlee Simpson have had different paths in Hollywood over the years. Jessica Simpson has amassed a $200 million fortune through her music career and successful fashion line worth $1 billion, while her sister worked on her music career before becoming a reality show star and gained a $9.5 million net worth.

Paris and Nicky Hilton

Paris and Nicky Hilton, born in a family with a successful business empire, were once known as socialites and great-granddaughters of the founder of the Hilton Hotel chain. The sisters have earned their living, with Paris being the highest-paid female DJ globally, earning $1 million per gig, as stated by Yahoo Finance and having a $300 million net worth. Her sister Nicky is a successful businesswoman with a $50 million fortune.

Beyonce And Solange Knowles

A gifted duo of sisters, Beyonce and Solange Knowles are prolific singers known for their powerful voices and strong performances. Beyonce has become a pop culture phenomenon as a fashion icon, businesswoman, and singer who has earned a $440 million fortune, while her sister is a singer and activist with a $9 million net worth.

Mary-Kate And Ashley Olsen

The Olsen twins were only seven months old when they worked on the television show Full House. After taking a break from acting, the duo sold their lucrative fashion line The Row to Saks Fifth Avenue and earned $100-$200 million annually. The sisters have a $500 million combined net worth.

Kylie And Kendall Jenner

The Jenner sisters are known to have sibling fights on screen; however, they always have encouraged each other, as reported by Teen Vogue. Kylie and Kendall have evolved into successful businesswomen and fashion icons in the last few years and amassed $900 million and $45 million fortunes, respectively.

Other notable sibling duos include Vanessa and Stella Hudgens and Emily and Zooey Deschanel. These sister duos have always ensured to put their family before fame and fortune and supported each other through every obstacle faced. While being a pillar for one another, they have established themselves as successful artists in the industry.
