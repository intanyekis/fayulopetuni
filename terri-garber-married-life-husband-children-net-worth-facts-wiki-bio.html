Who is Terri Garber?

Terri Garber started her acting career with her appearance on the daytime soap opera Texas as Allison Linden in 1982. In 1984, she made her first feature film role in the action movie Toy Soldiers. However, she came to limelight with her role as Southern belle Ashton Main in the ABC miniseries North and South: Book II in 1985. Furthermore, the miniseries is the seventh highest-rated miniseries in television history.

However, to date, the love birds are living a happy and luxurious life with each other. In addition, she is also a pet lover, who consists of two puppies and a cat.
