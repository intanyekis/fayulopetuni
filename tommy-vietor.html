Biography

Tommy Vietor was born in Dedham, MA on August 31, 1980. Political commentator who has gained fame as a host of the popular podcasts Pod Save America and Pod Save the World. He co-founded the podcasts’ Crooked Media production company in January 2017, and served as a spokesperson for the UN Security Council and Barack Obama between 2011-2013. He has featured Tim Kaine as a guest on his podcast Pod Save the World. He graduated from Kenyon College with his BA in philosophy in 2002. Following graduation, he worked with then-Illinois state senator Barack Obama on his first statewide run for office as deputy press secretary.
