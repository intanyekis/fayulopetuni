Formation of Dokken and Success

In the late 1970s, Don Dokken embarked on an exhilarating musical journey by forming the band Dokken. It was in 1981 that Dokken achieved a major breakthrough with their album “Breakin’ the Chains.” This album showcased the band’s raw talent and distinctive style, establishing them as a force to be reckoned with in the world of glam metal. The album’s title track became an instant hit, propelling Dokken into the spotlight and garnering critical acclaim.

Driven by their relentless pursuit of musical excellence, Dokken continued to release successful albums and captivate audiences with their electrifying live performances. As the band gained popularity, they achieved chart-topping success with albums like “Tooth and Nail,” “Under Lock and Key,” and “Back for the Attack.”
