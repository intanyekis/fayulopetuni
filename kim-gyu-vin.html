Kim Gyu-vin is a South Korean singer, musician, performer, and television personality who is known as a member of the boy band Zerobaseone (abbreviated as ZB1). Managed by Wake One Entertainment, the group was formed through Mnet’s reality competition show Boys Planet in 2023. Kim Gyu-vin was revealed as one of the contestants on the show in 2022 and was ranked 7th in the finale, thus becoming a member of Zerobaseone.

Kim Gyu-vin Facts

  • He was chosen by trainees as ‘Shiny Boys’ and was then revealed as one of the contestants on the then-upcoming survival show Boys Planet on December 26, 2022. A few days later, on December 29, Kim Gyu-vin was officially revealed as a contestant on Boys Planet (representing Yue Hua Entertainment).
  • Before appearing as a contestant on Boys Planet in 2023, he was a trainee for 3 years and 11 months.
  • His fellow Zerobaseone members include Sung Han-bin, Kim Ji-woong, Zhang Hao, Seok Matthew, Kim Tae-rae, Ricky, Han Yu-jin, and Park Gun-wook. The group’s name represents the “journey of the nine trainees to be completed as one after their debut” and refers to the “glorious beginning” of the 9 members.
  • Kim Gyu-vin’s role models are ATEEZ‘s San and Hongjoong and EXO‘s Kai.
  • The body parts he is the most confident about are his eyes, nose, mouth, arms, and legs.
  • Some of his hobbies include playing games and eating delicious food.
  • Kim Gyu-vin speaks Korean as well as a little bit of English.
  • His specialties are balancing and losing rock-paper-scissors.
  • In July 2023, it was revealed that Kim Gyu-vin had decided to take a break from his activities due to health concerns.
