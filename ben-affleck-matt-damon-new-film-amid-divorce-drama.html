Ben Affleck Partners Up With Matt Damon For New Film Amid Divorce Drama

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, May 31, 2024

Highlights

  • Ben Affleck and Matt Damon are reuniting to star in a new crime thriller, along with producing the film under their joint production banner.
  • Despite rumors surrounding their relationship, Ben is focusing on work commitments while Jennifer Lopez is focusing on her career.
  • Matt Damon reportedly never liked J.Lo and may be relieved to see Ben moving on if the rumors suggesting their separation are true.

Ben Affleck has a new movie to distract himself from his alleged break-up from Jennifer Lopez, and it’s got him reuniting with his long-time friend and collaborator, Matt Damon.

Ben and Matt announced their collaboration in the upcoming crime thriller film, RIP. The project will be produced under their joint production banner, Artists Equity, and will be directed by Joe Carnahan.

Filming is scheduled to commence later this fall, while details about the plot have not yet been disclosed. The film is currently in talks to sell distribution rights to both streaming platforms and theaters.

Ben and Matt have a long history of collaborating together. They recently appeared together in the 2022 basketball drama Air , with Ben also taking on the role of director,

The pair rose to fame together by co-writing the acclaimed drama Good Will Hunting in 1997. Their screenplay earned them the Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay, while the late Robin Williams received the Oscar for Best Supporting Actor for his role in the film.

Their other film collaborations include Dogma, School Ties, and most recently, The Last Duel.
