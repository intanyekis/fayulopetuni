Rumer Willis Gets Celebratory Dancing With the Stars Tattoo

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Rumer Willis will forever have her Dancing With the Stars win. Demi Moore and Bruce Willis’ eldest daughter, who scored the season 20 Mirrorball Trophy, got a tattoo in honor of the hit ABC competition show on Tuesday, June 2.

“Thank you @dr_woo_ssc for this beautiful art. I got this as a reminder that what we are searching for can only be found within yourself…not from your job, how much money you make, who you’re dating, how many followers you have. The value and love you have for yourself can only come from within and I am so grateful to have had such an incredible experience to help me understand that,” the dancing champ wrote alongside her new bicep ink, which consists of two people dancing.

Willis is not putting her dancing shoes away yet either. The 26-year-old star has joined the show’s Dancing With the Stars: Live! Perfect Ten tour, along with partner Val Chmerkovskiy.

In a press release for the tour, which kicks off on June 13, Willis revealed she fell in love with dancing while competing.

“Val and I were lucky enough to have incredible fans that supported us throughout the entire show, and they are the reason we won,” her statement read. “I can't wait to meet the fans and perform for them live."
