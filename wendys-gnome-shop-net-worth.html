Introduction The Wendy’s Gnome Shop

The Founder Of Wendy’s Gnome Shop

Wendy Hoffmeister is the Founder, and CEO of Wendy’s Gnome Shop discovered her passion for crafting gnomes while working as a labor and delivery nurse in North Carolina. Despite having a successful career in nursing, she started the business in November 2019 as a hobby.

The idea of making gnomes came to her after a breakup, and she created her first-ever gnome to lift her spirits.

Wendy’s daughter Amber is a nurse and a full-time gnome maker at the business. Wendy worked as a registered nurse for six years, including two years at Tampa General Hospital and four years at Kaiser Permanente, where she also served as an assistant nurse manager for two years.
