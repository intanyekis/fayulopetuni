Adrianna Di Liello is a Canadian actress who rose to fame after having been cast as Lithpy in the 2014 horror-thriller musical film, Stage Fright, and as Jenna Cristinziano in the TV drama series, Backstage. She has also appeared in the sci-fi family series, Annedroids, and in a variety of short movies as well. Adrianna has garnered a huge fan base with more than 50k followers on Instagram.
