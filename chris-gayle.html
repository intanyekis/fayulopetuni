Biography

Chris Gayle was born in Kingston, Jamaica on September 21, 1979. Captain of the West Indies’ Test side from 2007 to 2010, during which time he recorded two triple-centuries. He set a number of records during his career, once recording the highest individual T20 score (175 not out) in a match. He and Kumar Sangakkara played together on the Jamaica Tallawahs of the Caribbean Premier League. He played with Kingston’s renowned Lucas Cricket Club, which he said saved him from a life on the streets.
