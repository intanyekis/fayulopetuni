Christine Brown's Love Life Dramatically Changed After Her Marriage To Kody Brown Ended

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, June 24, 2024

Highlights

  • Christine Brown, ex-wife of Kody Brown, found love again after leaving her polygamous lifestyle.
  • She introduced her new partner, David Woolley, in 2023 and got engaged shortly after.
  • Christine and David married in October 2023, and she praised him for his integrity and kindness.

Sister Wives star Christine Brown’s love life took a different turn after her marriage with Kody Brown ended. Christine was Kody’s third wife and their love story played out on their family’s famous reality series on TLC. Christine remained in her plural family for 25 years, welcoming six children with Kody. However, she decided it was time to say goodbye to that chapter of her life in 2021.

The TV star had quite a transformation after ending her spiritual marriage. Following their messy split, Christine found love again, and it was nothing like her polygamous marriage to Kody. The mom-of-six did not opt for a spiritual marriage; neither was she the third wife. This article covers Christine’s whirlwind love story after her polygamous lifestyle and who she moved on with after her marriage with Kody went down the drain.

Inside Christine Brown And David Woolley’s Marriage Following Her Kody Brown Relationship

Christine introduced her new love, David Woolley, to the world in 2023 after her marriage with Kody Brown ended, and she couldn’t stop sharing several heartfelt messages about her newfound love. The former “Sister Wives” star announced that she was back in the dating game in early 2023 with a post on social media where she wrote:

“I’m dating again!! Holy. Hell. Awkward. Dating online is crazy!... Any advice for dating at 50?!”

Less than a month after the post, Christine went public about her relationship with David, and it was clear that she was totally smitten by him. She gushed about her new lover on social media, explaining how happy she had been since meeting David. On Valentine’s Day in 2023, Christine took to social media to gush about David with a series of images featuring both of them and a caption:

“I finally found the love of my life, David. The first time he held me close, it felt like my soul took its first breath. He’s wonderful and kind, incredible with my children, and an adorable grandpa. I never dreamed I could find a love like this.”

Their love story took a rather swift turn as they met each other's families. David has eight children from his previous marriage. Christine also shares children Aspyn, Mykelti, Paedon, Gwendlyn, Ysabel, and Truely with her ex-husband Kody. Christine’s children lived in different parts of the United States, but this was not a challenge for David as he made sure he met all of them. He also met some of her fellow Sister Wives stars' children and grandchildren. Fans were shocked by Kody Brown's reaction to David after they met.

Two months after Christine went public about her new relationship, the reality TV star revealed she and David were on the next level. Christine shared her joy on social media on April 13, 2023, with photos of her and David as she explained that they got engaged. In the post, Christine noted that David had popped the question in a very romantic way. David also shared his excitement, noting that he was the luckiest man and Christine was the most caring person he had ever met.

A few months after their engagement, the lovebirds tied the knot on October 7, 2023. Christine had her dream wedding in Moab, Utah, in front of 330 guests. The Sister Wives alum revealed to People exclusively that the ceremony was everything she thought she couldn’t have. For the TV star, walking down the aisle was a dream come true.

Their wedding ceremony began with a lovely dinner beside the Colorado River a day before the set wedding date.

Christine, who wore a white dress with a plunging neckline from Boda Bridal, was escorted to the altar by her son, Paedon Brown, and her father. After saying their vows, the couple joined their guest for the reception party under a tent filled with spectacular lights. The nuptials aired on Sister Wives as a special.

After tying the knot, Christine shared nothing but admirable details of her new husband. The TV star told People in September 2023 that David had more integrity than anyone she had ever met. She explained that David’s word was his bond, and he always kept to his word. The doting wife also stated

"He’s a phenomenal person to the core. He’s just outstanding. Yeah, I just feel like I won a gold mine. I won the lottery with him."

The TLC star elaborated on how different her second marriage was, noting that life was so simple, and she was certain she had found her soulmate. Christine’s second husband, David, who she met on a dating app, is in the construction business. David, who is both a parent and grandparent like Christine, opened up about his occupation in a July 2023 post on his social media. According to the post, he has been in the construction business for over 40 years, working as a drywall installer since 1980, and has owned his company for about 20 years.

David’s social media show how much of a family-oriented man he is. His social media boasts photos of him with his grandchildren at different moments. He also posted photos of his adventures with Christine and their other exciting moments together.

Why Did Christine Brown And Kody Brown’s Marriage End?

The Sister Wives stars have experienced lots of drama, which led to messy splits previously foretold by a close friend of the family. The show’s patriarch, Kody, married his first wife, Meri Brown, his second wife, Janelle Brown, and his third wife, Christine, spiritually. Robyn Brown, his fourth wife, became his only legally married wife in 2010.

Christine and Kody got married in 1994, and they announced that the marriage was over in November 2021. Before their breakup, Christine revealed that she and Kody grew apart before they eventually called it quits. However, she stated that they would continue to maintain a strong presence in each other’s lives because of their children. It is no secret that Christine and Kody grew apart gradually as some of the drama that caused their estrangement played out on TLC.

During the coronavirus outbreak, Kody restrained movements in his home, which kept the large family apart. He revealed in a Thanksgiving episode of Sister Wives in 2021 that Covid kept a strain on his family. However, Christine found Kody’s decision hypocritical, and she questioned him in another episode, noting that Kody allowed Robyn’s nanny in the home.

During an episode of the TLC family reality series, Christine also shared the surprises she discovered during a therapy program with Kody. The mom-of-six did not reveal what she and Kody had discussed during their therapy, but she noted that he had held grudges longer than she did.

It also appeared that Kody Brown's marriage to Robyn contributed to the former couple’s split. Christine and the other sister wives were not close to Robyn, and Kody was not pleased with this. In an episode of their show, Christine revealed that it was hard seeing Kody's functional marriages with the other wives while she struggled.

Christine was open about her failed marriage with Kody. During the season 17 premiere of Sister Wives, Kody told Christine that he was no longer attracted to her, and this appeared to be the last straw for Christine. She revealed in an interview that it was embarrassing to hear that her husband was no longer attracted to her, and she knew the marriage was over after his statement.

When Christine and her husband of 25 years decided to call it quits, she revealed it was a big relief. Kody, on the other hand, stated in the season 17 premiere that Christine had already decided the marriage was over by the time their conversation aired on the show. He also claimed she was a game player.

Kody later admitted the role he played in their failed marriage. According to the Sister Wives star, he had wanted to break his marriage with Christine before she initiated their split. Kody noted that he was glad Christine was brave enough to initiate their split. He also confessed that he wasn’t in love with Christine.
