If you haven’t heard by now, Disney + is launching a new version of Cheaper by the Dozen, available March 18 and a bit different from previous versions.

The original 1950 film featured Clifton Webb and Jeanne Crain as parents to 12 children. Back in 1950, the vast majority of the cast, including all 12 child actors were uncredited in the film. The 2003 film starred comedian Steve Martin and Bonnie Hunt. The 12 children included well-known actors and actresses, Piper Perabo, Tom Welling, Hilary Duff among others.

What makes the 2022 version different?

According to The Things, Union and Zach Braff star as the couple, showing audiences what families in 2022 look like today. Mom and Dad both bring five children from previous relationships and only 10 children make up the ‘dozen’ in the film.

Check out who will star in the remake and their current net worth today.

Gabrielle Union – $40 Million

Union plays Zoey Baker, the mother of a brood of 10. In real life, Union shares her net worth with NBA star Dwayne Wade. She’s appeared in popular films such as She’s All That, 10 Things I Hate About You and Bring it On. The 49-year-old actress has worked with Disney in the past, voicing the character of Nala in Disney’s The Lion Guard.

Zach Braff – $20 Million

Best known for his role in television’s Scrubs, Braff will star as Paul Baker, husband to Union’s character and father to 10 children. According to Celebrity Net Worth, Braff has a wide range of talent, starring in television, film and as a screenwriter and director. He wrote and directed Garden State, with a successful debut in the box office and Braff won a Grammy Award for Best Compilation Soundtrack for a Motion Picture.

Ericka Christensen – $10 Million

She’s known for roles in television’s Parenthood from 2010 to 2015, films like Traffic, Swimfan and others but in 2022’s version of Cheaper by the Dozen, the 39-year-old Christensen will play Kate, Paul’s ex-wife and the Bakers’ babysitter.

Timon Kyle Durrett – $ 1 Million

Durrett has been around since 1999, taking roles in films like Platinum Playaz, Everyday But Christmas and Her Only Choice. He’s probably best known for his role as Davis West on Ava DuVarney’s Queen Sugar. Durrett will play Dom Clayton, ex-husband of Zoey and father to five of her ten children.

Luke Prael – $1 Million (Approximate)

In another modern family casting, Prael is Paul’s nephew Seth, living with the family. Prior to the 2022 film, Prael appeared in Eighth Grade and Boarding School.

Andre Robinson – $800,000

Taking the role of DJ Baker, oldest son of Union’s character, will be a big change for Robinson. He has a long history of voice work, including Clyde McBride on The Loud House since 2018 and has lent his voice to films like Hotel Transylvania 2 and Despicable Me 2. This time, Robinson will be taking on the big screen, showing off his talent for new fans.

Kylie Rogers – $500,000

Rogers plays Ella Baker, the oldest of Paul’s brood in Cheaper by the Dozen, but fans have been following her as Minx Lawrence in The Whispers and Yellowstone as the young version of Beth. While the 18-year-old has appeared in a handful of films, it’s possible this will be her break-out role.

Aryan Simhadri – $200,000

The 15-year-old Asian-American actor is best known for his role as Rohan in Disney’s 2021 film Spin. His role in the family remake will be that of Haresh Baker. As an amateur magician, critics imagine Simhadri will have a few surprises up his sleeve for his new role on the big screen.

Caylee Blosenski – $100,00

According to IMDb, she’s an athlete and qualifier for Nationals as well as a passionate actress. In her film debut, however, Blosenski playing Harley, another daughter in the clan. Blosenski is one to watch in Hollywood. She reportedly was diagnosed with cancer in the fifth grade has a prosthetic leg but her challenges in life haven’t stopped her yet.

Leo Abelo Perry – $100,000 (Approximate)

Perry plays Luka, twin to Luna (Mykal-Michelle Harris) and adds some fun and mischief to the film. Perry is best known for his activism. He co-found The Activators, a kid-designed podcast to discuss issues important to youth. He also formed the organization Dance for Justice and raised $20,000 for the Black Lives Matter movement.

Mykal-Michelle Harris – $100,000 (Approximate)

Just getting started in her career, 10-year-old Harris has appeared in 2019’s Mixed-ish and 2017’s Big Little Lies. She plays Luna, twin to Leo Abelo Perry’s character in the film.

Christian and Sebastian Cote

Two real-life brothers were cast as Bailey and Bronx Baker in the film. Cheaper by the Dozen will be the twins’ feature film debut but they have been seen in Hulu’s Monsterland recently.

Journee Brown

Brown plays the oldest of Zoey’s children, Deja Baker. She’s probably best known for her role in televisions Orange is the New Black but she’s about to make her film debut on the big screen.
