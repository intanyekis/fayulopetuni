Bianca Censori's friends 'stage intervention' over Kanye West marriage

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

Explore More

Wake up, Mrs. West.

Bianca Censori’s friends and family in her native Australia allegedly staged an “intervention” with her amid rumored fears that Kanye West is controlling her life.

Sources tell the Daily Mail that her loved ones are now confident that Censori, 28, is aware of 46-year-old West’s “controlling way” after they allegedly told her to “wake the f–k up” last week during her first trip Down Under in nearly a year.

The Yeezy architect reportedly spent some family time with her parents Leo and Alexandra and sister Angeline, with whom she enjoyed a night out on the town at Melbourne’s bar Marquis of Lorne.

“No one made a fuss at all,” one insider told the outlet about the famous face’s incognito night. “She was with her sister and they just blended in with everyone else — even though her outfit was sort of on the outrageous side.”

“I didn’t even recognize who she was until a mate of mine pointed her out and said, ‘Look, that’s Kayne’s missus over there.'”

The source continued, “She was there just having a good time and a few drinks, catching up with friends. It was really no big deal.”

The Post has contacted reps for West for comment.

Censori and the “Heartless” rapper married on Dec. 20 and have been making headlines around the world ever since.

The former fashion mogul has been accused by onlookers of embarrassing and exposing Censori by dressing her in head-to-toe sheer hosiery — in one outing, covering her breasts with only a square purple pillow.

Sources previously alleged to the Daily Mail that West had also instructed Censori to “never speak,” as well as follow his rules about only eating specific foods.

In August, West was infamously caught with his pants down with Censori lurking in his nether region while on a water taxi ride in Venice, Italy.
