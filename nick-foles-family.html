Who Is Nick Foles Family? NFL Quarterbacks Wife &amp; Kids

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 17, 2024

Nick Foles is a famous quarterback for the NFL, and people were recently searching for Nick Foles’ family as they were keen to know more about him. 

Nick is an American football player who plays for the NFL’s Chicago Bears. His full name is Nicholas Edward Foles, and he was born on January 20, 1989, in Austin, Texas. 

In 2007, the NFL quarterback graduated from Westlake High school and later studied at a college in Michigan state for one year. 

Then, he took a transfer to the University of Arizona. Just after completing his schooling, Nick began playing football. He has completed his Master’s degree in Divinity. 

Who are Nick Foles’ wife and daughter?

According to sources, it was confirmed that Nick Foles married Tori Moore, in 2014.

In 2017, the couple welcomed their daughter, Lily James Foles. 

For those who don’t know about Nick’s wife, she is an athlete, and her sisters are Evan Moore and Ryan Moore.

It was reported that Nick and Tori met each other while they were studying at the University of Arizona.

Nick Foles Family

Nick Foles was born to his parents, Larry Foles, his father, and Melissa Foles, his mother.

However, information regarding his parents is not available on the internet. If there is any information, then this section will be updated. 

Nick has two siblings, and he happens to be the only son of his parents. He is the brother of two beautiful sisters whose names are Lacey and Katie Foles. 

Nick Foles’ career

In 2006, Nick began his professional career when he started playing with the Michigan State team.

He was selected by Philadelphia Eagles in the third round of the NFL in 2012. Five years later, in 2017, he led his team to win their first Super Bowl LII.

In March 2017, he signed a 2-year contract with Philadelphia Eagles. Later, Nick shared his autobiography, “Believe It: My journey of success, failure, and overcoming the odds.”

In an interview, he said after his retirement, he would like to be a pastor.
