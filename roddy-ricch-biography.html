Roddy Ricch Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Who is Roddy Ricch?

American Roddy Ricch is a Grammy Award-winning rapper, singer-songwriter, and record producer. Ricch is renowned as the record producer of the track, Ballin, and the album, Feed Tha Streets II. 

He happens to be the most nominated artist at the 2020 American Music Awards with eight nominations.

When did Roddy Ricch born?

Roddy Ricch was born Rodrick Wayne Moore, Jr. on 22nd October 1998 in Compton, California. He is of All-American ethnicity.

To date, he has not shared any details on his family background including his father, mother, and siblings.

Moving towards his childhood, he started rapping and singing at the age of 8. He spent his youth in Atlanta, Georgia. Later, he started making beats by the age of 16.

He completed his High School education at the Carson Senior High School. Also, he joined the Westchester Enriched Sciences Magnets.

Roddy Ricch- Professional Career

– In November 2017, Roddy Ricch launched his first mixtape, Feed The Streets. The mixtape includes 17 tracks including Free Game, Uber, HoodRicch, and so on.

Through the mixtape, he earned the attention of rappers including Meek Mill, DJ Mustard, and 03 Greedo.

– In March 2018, he released his EP, Be 4 Tha Fame. In July 2018, in collaboration with the rapper, London Tyler Holmes, he launched the single, Die Young dedicated to his childhood friend.

The single became a milestone in his singing career that had over 20 million views on youtube.

– Following that, he launched the track, Project Dreams with DJ, Marshmello. The track peaked at the 28th position in the US Rhythmic (Billboard).

– In June 2019, he produced the track, Ballin with record producer, Mustard for Mustard’s album, Perfect Ten. The track peaked at the 12th position in the charting song list.

– On 6th December 2019, he released his debut album, Please Excuse Me for Being Antisocial with 16 tracks. The album spent four weeks on the Billboard 200 list. Also, it received a Platinum certification from RIAA.

– In early 2020, he appeared in various albums like Wunna and the song, Walk Em Down and Rockstar.

Awards & Nominations

  • 2020- Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance.
  • 2020- Nominated for American Music Awards 8x times under the categories like Artist of the Year, New Artist of the Year, Collaboration of the Year, Favorite Song, Favorite Male Artist, Favorite Album, and Favorite Song (2x Rap & Hip Hop)

How much is the net worth of Roddy Ricch?

As of 2020, he has an estimated net worth of $10 million. His earnings as a singer are in the range of $22k to $209k. Besides that, as a songwriter, he makes in the range of $29k – $122k.

Apart from that, he also earns through the royalties of his music.

Body Measurements- Height & Weight

Roddy Ricch has brown eyes with black hair. He stands at a height of 5 feet 7 inches and weighs around 64 kg.

His chest and waist sizes are 40, and 32 inches respectively. Also, his shoe size is 9.5 (US).

Roddy Ricch- Controversy & Rumors

Back in 2019, he was arrested for felony domestic violence charges. As per the LA County Sheriff’s Department arrest record, he was arrested on 11th August 2019 at 4:50 a.m.

As per some online reports, the verbal argument between him and his girlfriend turned to be a physical one. However, neither of them required any type of medical attention.

Social Media

Roddy has over 3 m followers on Twitter, 5 m on Facebook, and 6.4m on Instagram.

On Instagram, he is only following the rapper, Nipsey Hussle.

Also, he has a self-titled YouTube channel with 4.81 million subscribers with more than 1.69 billion views.
