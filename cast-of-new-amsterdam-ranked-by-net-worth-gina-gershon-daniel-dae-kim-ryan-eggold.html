The Cast Of 'New Amsterdam' Ranked By Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 4, 2024

New Amsterdam is an incredible medical drama, based on the book Twelve Patients: Life and Death at Bellevue Hospital that was written by Eric Manheimer. It will probably be a fun show for all the Grey's Anatomy fans out there. This show is not only enthralling because of its great plot but also because of the very talented cast that makes it happen.

All of the amazing actors that are responsible for the success of the show have worked on other projects and have built their fortunes with their talent and hard work. Let's see how much these actors are currently worth.

10 Sendhil Ramamurthy - $500,000

Sendhil Ramamurthy portrayed a recurring character on the show, Dr. Akash Panthaki. He was the boyfriend of Dr. Helen Sharpe, who was the head of Oncology, and former Deputy Medical Director. He appeared on several episodes of the first season. He currently has a $500 thousand net worth. Throughout his career, he has worked a lot in theater, and he has also appeared in the movie In the Beginning, the TV series Ultimate Force, and starred in Orient Express.

9 Tyler Labine - $2 Million

Tyler Labine portrays Dr. Iggy Frome in New Amsterdam. Frome is one of the main characters, the Head of Psychiatry at the Medical Center, and a very well-respected professional. Tyler is currently worth $2 million, and this net worth is the result of his incredible career. Some of his most important jobs were in the TV shows Breaker High, Invasion and Reaper, and the movie Tucker & Dale vs. Evil. He also had the lead role of Scotty on the series Dead Last and ABC series That Was Then.

8 Michelle Forbes - $3 Million

Michelle Forbes joined the cast on season 4 as Dr. Veronica Fuentes, and her character was described as "a calm, poised and fearless fixer who is brought in to ‘reconstruct’ the failing hospital," and planned to "wipe clean the inept board."

Michelle is a very experienced, talented actress that has worked on many amazing projects such as Orphan Black,The Hunger Games: Mockingjay - Part 2, Confessions of an American Girl, Dandelion, etc. She is currently worth $3 million.

7 Ryan Eggold - $5 Million

Max Goodwin, the protagonist of the show, arrived at New Amsterdam Medical Center as the new Medical Director and took it upon himself to drastically change the way the hospital worked, to make it more efficient and help more people. That, of course, caused tension with some of the team, but Max persevered. The man behind such an interesting character is Ryan Eggold, an actor with a $5 million net worth. Apart from New Amsterdam, he was also in shows like Brothers & Sisters, Veronica Mars, the Young and the Restless, and many more.

6 Janet Montgomery - $5 Million

Janet Montgomery, the great actress that plays Dr. Lauren Bloom, is worth $5 million. Despite being an amazing professional, Dr. Bloom has a difficult secret, which is her substance abuse. That eventually starts affecting her work, and she reaches out for help to get some healing on the trauma that caused her addiction in the first place.

Janet Montgomery's talent is evident in her portrayal, and that, combined with hard work, helped her build her net worth. She appeared on the show Entourage, worked on horror movies like The Hills Run Red, Wrong Turn 3: Left for Dead, and Dead Cert, and guest-starred in many shows like Skins, Merlin, and more.

5 Ron Rifkin - $6 Million

Peter Fulton, or Dean Fulton, was portrayed by Ron Rifkin, and he's the man responsible for bringing Max Goodwin to the Medical Center. He was the Dean of numerous hospitals, including New Amsterdam, and he knew that, in order for the Center to survive, it needed someone with fresh ideas. Unfortunately, because his focus was protecting the patients and not necessarily generating profit, he was fired. Ron Rifkin left the show in 2019, and is now worth $6 million. He has worked on projects such as Alias, Brothers & Sisters, and L.A. Confidential.

4 Daniel Dae Kim - $10 Million

Daniel Dae Kim played Cassian Shin, a trauma surgeon at the New Amsterdam Medical Center that made his first appearance at the end of the second season of the show. 3 Gina Gershon - $10 Million

Jeanie Bloom was "a raging alcoholic throughout most of her daughter’s childhood," read the character description. "She would get mean and then sick, while Lauren was left to clean up the mess. This happened as early as age 7, which was the first time Lauren got drunk. She chugged half of Jeanie’s martini in the hopes it would keep her sober. It didn’t."

Gina Gershon had a recurring role in New Amsterdam as Jeanie Bloom, Dr. Lauren Bloom's complicated mother. This was only one of the countless incredible roles she played that resulted in her $10 million net worth. She appeared in Brooklyn Nine-Nine, Life on Mars, Melrose Place, and Dreamland, just to name a few.

2 Anupam Kher - $70 Million

On top of the list is the man that plays the Head of Neurology at New Amsterdam Medical Center, Dr. Vijay Kapoor: none other than Anupam Kher. The actor currently has a staggering $70 million net worth, which he built not only through his work in blockbusters like Silver Livings Playbook and many Bollywood projects, he's also a very accomplished director and was the Chairman of the Censor Board and National School of Drama in India. Apart from his acting career, another occupation he has is his job as a motivational speaker.
