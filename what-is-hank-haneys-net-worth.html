Hank Haney Wiki Biography

Born on August 24th, 1955 in Lake Forest, Illinois, USA, Hank Haney is a renowned professional golf instructor and coach. He is widely known for his work with acclaimed golfers such as Mark O’Meara and Tiger Woods. Haney also owns and operates four training centers in the Dallas, Texas area and has been the star of his own reality TV show, “The Haney Project,” where he helps celebrities enhance their golf skills.

Ever wondered about the wealth accumulated by this golf maestro? The estimated net worth of Hank Haney has soared to over $10 million as of early 2017, garnered from his extensive career spanning more than 40 years in golf coaching. He has coached over 200 professional golf players, thereby solidifying his financial status.

Hank Haney Net Worth $10 million

Hank’s fascination with golf began during his teenage years when he attended the University of Tulsa on a golf scholarship and obtained his degree in Education in 1977. Haney’s journey into the world of professional golf coaching commenced in 1976 under the tutelage of globally renowned instructors John Jacobs and Jim Hardy. Over the years, he held various significant positions at major golf facilities and operations, a foundation that significantly bolstered his net worth.

In 1991, Haney ventured into establishing his first training center. Currently, he owns and operates four top-tier golf training facilities in Texas and has authored several books and videos, elevating his popularity and wealth.

Not only has Hank Haney coached over 200 golfers competing in various tours, but he has also been involved in TV series, book publications, and a reality show, “The Haney Project,” all of which have substantially contributed to his financial prosperity.

For his dedication and coaching prowess, Haney has received numerous accolades and awards, further solidifying his place in the golf industry. In his personal life, Hank has been married to former model and occasional actress Suzanne Rogers since 2009.
