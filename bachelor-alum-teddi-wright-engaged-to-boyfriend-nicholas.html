Bachelor Alum Teddi Wright Engaged to Boyfriend Nicholas!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Source: Cosmopolitan

  • Former Bachelor contestant Teddi Wright is now engaged!
  • The 26-year-old announced her engagement to her boyfriend Nicholas on Instagram.

Bachelor Alum Teddi Wright is now engaged to boyfriend Nicholas

Teddi Wright is now engaged!

The former Bachelor contestant announced her engagement to boyfriend Nicholas on 6th April 2023.

She shared the news with her followers on Instagram with a video. The reality star also included a glimpse at her Jean Dousset diamond ring alongside a montage of their joyous moments together.

She captioned the post:

“So this is love … ??. I am engaged and feel so blessed I get to spend forever with my best friend :’) I’ve been crying for days since it’s happened and it still feels like a dream.”

Wright then praised her fiance’s engagement ring choice:

“Can we take a moment for how good my fiancé (omg) and @jeandousset did on the ring.”

She ended it by saying:

“I feel like the luckiest girl in the world!! Now let’s get me married ??”

Teddi Wright has maintained a private relationship with Nicholas

Teddi Wright first announced her relationship with Nicholas in January 2023 on New Year’s Day.

She captioned the video:

“First new years kiss ever!! Happy new year #2023.”

It featured moments of the couple showing each other some love and affection.

Since then, she had stayed tight-lipped about their relationship but she did share a post on how her fiance gave her “butterflies” and clinked their wine glasses.

In an Instagram Q and A session, someone asked about her boyfriend and she wrote:

“He doesn’t have social media and he doesn’t fully understand this world but he tries his best to be supportive and take a million pictures lol.”

She added:

“Will try to get him on camera more but wanted to protect our relationship in the beginning! I think the kids call this ‘private but not secret.'”

Teddi Wright in The Bachelor

Wright appeared on season 26 of The Bachelor from January to March 2022. She made her reality TV debut on the show but got eliminated in week 7.

Later, she participated in season 8 of Bachelor in Paradise to find love again but left the show in tears during the second week after accepting a rose from Andrew Spencer.

In an episode in October 2022, she expressed her desire to get married and have children. She said during the episode:

“I want to get married, and I want to have kids. … I want to go home and move past this and find that person.”

Days after her exit aired, she took to Instagram to express how proud she is of her own growth:

“1. Leaving environments that are cruel to me & the people around me. 2. Sticking to my boundaries no matter how many times people in authority try to cross them,” she wrote. “Excited for this next chapter and what the rest of the year will bring.”

 

Despite Spencer’s attempts to reconnect with her after the show, Wright hinted on her Instagram story in December 2022 that she had found a new boyfriend and referred to him as a “sweet lil boy.”

She later made their relationship public after celebrating the new year together.
