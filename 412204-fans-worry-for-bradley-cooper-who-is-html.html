Bradley Cooper and ex Irina Shayk share a daughter, Lea De Seine. The pair started dating in 2015 and had their only child in 2017. They broke up in 2019, a split that was so publicized that it led to Shayk's increased publicity.

After their breakup, the couple remained friends to co-parent their daughter amicably. Cooper and Shayk live blocks away from each other in the West Village in New York City so that they can share parental responsibilities.

Bradley Cooper, Irina Shayk, and Lea De Seine on October 4, 2018 in New York City | Source: Getty Images

Shayk said her ex was a full-on and hands-on dad as he didn't have a nanny. Lea once went on holiday for two weeks with her dad, and Shayk didn't have to call them even once. The Russian model said her daughter was being raised in privilege without the hard-knock Russian values she credits for her own strength.

Bradley Cooper in New York City on May 01, 2023 | Source: Getty Images

However, Shayk said she and Cooper are very strict parents. When Lea finishes eating, she gets up from the table, takes her plate with her, and says thank you. Shayk and Cooper have raised Lea to understand that she won't get anything without saying please and thank you.

Fans were concerned about Cooper's appearance and pointed out that he was lighter than usual. Some fans speculated that he lost weight because he was working on a secret project.

Bradley Cooper and Irina Shayk in New York City on February 7, 2019 | Source: Getty Images

On September 5, 2023, Shayk and Cooper put their co-parenting skills to work when they took their daughter for her first day of school in New York City. The pair held hands with their daughter as they walked her into the school compound to start her new school year.

The "Burnt" star wore a blue T-shirt, blue baseball cap, shorts, shades, and a pair of sneakers for the outing. Shayk was in a black sweatshirt dress and leather knee-high boots. The 37-year-old threw a hood over her dress in an attempt not to be recognized. Cooper and Shayk looked happy bringing their daughter to school.

Bradley Cooper and Lea in New York City on March 19, 2021 | Source: Getty Images

This outing isn't the first time the pair have shown up for their child. The trio went on an Italian vacation together a week before the school opening date. Pictures taken on the vacation showed Cooper and Shayk being cozy together.

Bradley Cooper, Irina Shayk, and Lea in Venice, Italy on August 30, 2018 | Source: Getty Images

The actor held onto Shayk's purse and helped her on and off a boat during a ride. He also put his hands around the model while they were grabbing a bite. After Cooper's public appearance while taking his daughter to school, he was spotted in another outing, and his physical outlook had fans worrying about him.
