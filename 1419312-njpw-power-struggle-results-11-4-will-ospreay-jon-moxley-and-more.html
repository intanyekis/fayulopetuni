NJPW Power Struggle Results (11/4): Will Ospreay, Jon Moxley, And More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 10, 2024

On November 4, New Japan Pro-Wrestling presented NJPW Power Struggle from Osaka Prefectural Gymnasium (Edion Arena Osaka) in Osaka, Japan.

In the main event, Will Ospreay defended the IWGP United States Championship against Shota Umino. Elsewhere on the show, Jon Moxley battled Great-O-Khan.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • Ryusuke Taguchi, The DKC & Boltin Oleg def. DRAGON GATE (Strong Machine J, Mochizuki Jr. & Yoshiki Kato)
  • United Empire (Jeff Cobb & Callum Newman) def. Young Blood (Yuto Nakashima & Oskar Leube)
  • El DespeWato (El Desperado & Master Wato) def. BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Clark Connors & Drilla Moloney) and Musashi Komatsu (YOH & MUSASHI) and Ichiban Sweet Boys (Kosei Fujita & Robbie Eagles)
  • KUSHIDA, Kevin Knight & Tama Tonga def. Los Ingobernables De Japon (BUSHI, Shingo Takagi & Titan)
  • Los Ingobernables De Japon (Tetsuya Naito & Yota Tsuji) def. Just Five Guys (SANADA & Yuya Uemura)
  • David Finlay def. Tanga Loa
  • Jon Moxley vs. Great-O-Khan went to a double countout, and it was started as a Falls Count Anywhere match.
  •  Jon Moxley def. Great-O-Khan
  • Kazuchika Okada, Hiroshi Tanahashi & Tomohiro Ishii (c) def. TMDK (Zack Sabre Jr., Mikey Nicholls & Shane Haste)
  • Catch 2/2 (TJP & Francesco Akira) def. HOUSE OF TORTURE (SHO & Yoshinobu Kanemaru)
  • Hiromu Takahashi (c) def. Taiji Ishimori
  • Takahashi named El Desperado as his challenger at NJPW Wrestle Kingdom 18.
  • : Will Ospreay (c) def. Shota Umino
  • Jon Moxley confronted Ospreay after the match. David Finlay attacked them both and smashed the IWGP US and UK Championships.

Bryan Danielson has challenged Kazuchika Okada to a match at NJPW Wrestle Kingdom 18. More information is available here.
